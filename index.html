What I do

In a nutshell, I’m the guy who turns caffeine into code and ideas into reality. Here’s a closer look at what I bring to the table:

Web Development

I craft sleek, responsive websites that turn ideas into interactive digital experiences. From front-end wizardry with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to back-end sorcery with Spring Boot and Django, I ensure your online presence is as powerful as it is beautiful.

Mobile Development

On the go? I build mobile apps that keep you connected and engaged no matter where you are. Whether it's a native Android app or a cross-platform solution with Flutter, I bring your mobile vision to life with style and efficiency.

Data Engineering

Data doesn’t just speak to me it sings! I design and build robust data pipelines that not only transform and load data but also make it dance across dashboards in real-time. Apache Beam, Dataflow, BigQuery these are the tools of my trade, and I use them to turn raw data into valuable insights.

Agile & DevOps

I don’t just write code; I live and breathe Agile. From Scrum to Kanban, I’m all about iterative development and continuous improvement. Add in some DevOps magic with tools like Docker, Kubernetes, and GitLab CI, and you've got a recipe for rapid, reliable delivery..

Learning & Certifications

Continuous learning is my game. With certifications in Python, React, and Business Intelligence, among others, I’m always expanding my skill set to stay on top of industry trends and technologies.

Communication

Whether it's leading a project, coordinating with teams, or simply making sure everyone’s on the same page, I excel at keeping the conversation flowing. I bridge the gap between tech and people, ensuring that ideas are not just heard, but understood.
